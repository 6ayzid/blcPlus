# Privacy Policy for blcPlus

**Effective Date:** May 4, 2026

At **blcPlus**, we value your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our browser extension handles your information.

## 1. Information Collection and Storage
blcPlus is designed to automate the login process for the Daffodil International University (DIU) Blended Learning Center (BLC). To do this, the extension requires you to provide:
*   **Student ID / Username**
*   **Password**

**CRITICAL PRIVACY NOTE:** 
All credentials entered into the blcPlus dashboard are stored **locally** within your browser's secure storage API (`chrome.storage.local`). 

## 2. Information Usage
The stored information is used exclusively for:
*   Auto-filling the login form on `elearn.daffodilvarsity.edu.bd`.
*   Maintaining your session state to prevent automatic logouts.

## 3. Data Transmission
**blcPlus does NOT transmit your personal data to any external servers.** 
Your Student ID and Password never leave your local machine. The extension communicates only with the official Daffodil International University portal to facilitate your login.

## 4. Data Security
We utilize the browser's built-in extension storage, which is isolated from other websites and extensions. However, users are advised to keep their local machine secure to prevent unauthorized access to browser data.

## 5. Third-Party Access
We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your information to outside parties. No third-party analytics or tracking scripts are included in this extension.

## 6. User Control (Data Deletion)
You have full control over your data. You can delete your stored credentials at any time by:
1.  Opening the blcPlus popup.
2.  Clearing the input fields and clicking "Save Settings" or simply uninstalling the extension.
3.  Uninstalling the extension will automatically wipe all associated local storage data.

## 7. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the "Effective Date" at the top of this page.
